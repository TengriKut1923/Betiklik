Xbox Controller:
UP,DOWN,LEFT,RIGHT: D-Pad
NES button 'A' (Jump): 'A button'
NES button 'B' (Attack): 'X button'
NES button 'START' (Pause Menu): 'Start button'
NES button 'SELECT' (Cast Spell): 'Back button'

Keyboard Controls:
RIGHT:           'D' or             'ARROW RIGHT'
LEFT:               'A' or             'ARROW LEFT'
UP:                   'W' or            'ARROW UP'
DOWN:         'S' or              'ARROW DOWN'
NES button 'A' (Jump):  'SPACE BAR'
NES button 'B' (Attack): 'Ctrl'
NES button 'START' (Pause Menu): 'Enter'
NES button 'SELECT' (Cast Spell): 'T'

Options:
'O': Open Options Menu.
'M': Toggle music.
'N': Toggle sounds.

Window:
Fullscreen: 'F'
Windowed Scale Up:        hold 'Ctrl',                   press 'G'
Windowed Scale Down: hold 'Ctrl' + 'Shift', press 'G'

Quit App:
'Escape' (twice if in fullscreen)  or  hold Xbox Right Trigger + Xbox Left Trigger + Xbox Back(Select) + Xbox Start

Developer Tools:
To Activate/Deactivate: hold 'Ctrl' + 'Alt' + 'Shift', press 'Q'
'U': Toggle show app performance bar
'J': Toggle show XY points
'R': Toggle show hitboxes
'H': Toggle show solid collision points
'V': Toggle show HP numerical values
'K': Toggle show sprite outline
'L': Toggle show OG camera area

You can pause the app and advance frames one at a time.
Hold Left_Trigger+Right_Trigger+Right_Bumper and press Left_Bumper to pause the app. From there, pressing Right_Bumper will advance the frame. Holding Right_Bumper will run game normally but will pause app when released. Pressing Left_Bumper will unpause app(go back to normal).
