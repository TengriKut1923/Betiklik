Adventures of Demo is the TheXTech assets package. This is the unofficial spin-off from the A2XT series by Talkhaus. The featured episode (called "The first adventure") is a remix of "The Invasion" by Redigit, reworked by Wohlstand. This is one of two official assets packs used at releases of TheXTech itself.

Episodes

The first adventure

This is a remix of "The Invasion" by Redigit. It has various improvements, bug-fixes and additions such as multiple checkpoints.

Story

Five friends (Demo, her sister Iris, their Earthling friend Raocow, Kood, and Sheath) got suddenly warped into a weird and dark place. A Voice instructs them to find a door that leads into a big world whose inhabitants are in huge trouble: The evil Mr. Hell desires to take over the whole world and sent his army of various creatures and spirits to attack everyone. The locals ask our friends to help them defeat Mr. Hell and save their world from his malice.
